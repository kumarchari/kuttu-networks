About Kuttu Networks (P) LTD

Kuttu Networks (P) LTD is an Indian-based company dedicated to providing end-to-end solutions in telecom distribution, CCTV surveillance, website development, and technical support services for local ISPs and small-scale businesses.

Our mission is to empower industries, communities, and enterprises with robust network infrastructure, digital tools, and smart security systems — all under one roof. With a presence across Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, and Kerala, we’re not just delivering products — we’re enabling digital growth through reliable support, quality infrastructure, and cutting-edge technology.

We focus on four core service areas:

Telecom Products Distribution

We offer a wide range of certified, industry-standard products:

• Splicing Machines – Fujikura, INNO, Sumitomo, ATPL

• OTDRs, Power Meters, Cable Locators

• Fiber Optic Cables, Patch Cords, PLC Splitters

• HDPE Ducts, Joint Closures, Connectors

• Media Converters, OLTs, ONUs, Switches, SFPs

• FTTH & Cable TV accessories with pan-India delivery and installation support

CCTV Installation & Surveillance Solutions

We secure residential, commercial, and industrial spaces with:

• IP Cameras (2MP–8MP), DVR/NVR Systems

• Motion Detection, Cloud Monitoring, Thermal & Night Vision Cameras

• Biometric Devices, Boom Barriers, Entry Management

• 6-month free maintenance + same-day installation in select areas

Website Development for Industrial Clients

We build digital tools tailored to industrial needs:

• Custom Websites for Workshops & Factories

• GST-compliant Quotation Pages, Product Catalogs

• Multilingual Content, Google Business Setup

• WhatsApp, Call & Email Integration

• Complete domain, hosting & maintenance support

24/7 ISP Technical Support Services

We serve local ISPs and cable TV operators by offering:

• 24/7 NOC Monitoring

• Vendor & LCO Coordination

• Downtime Alerts & Root Cause Reports

• On-site & Remote Troubleshooting

• Monthly Network Health Reports
